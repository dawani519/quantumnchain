Frequently Asked Questions

How are profits paid out?

Profits are paid weekly directly to your account balance. You can withdraw these profits at any time or reinvest them for compound growth.

Can I withdraw my principal before the term ends?

Yes, you can withdraw your principal early, but an early withdrawal fee will apply. The fee varies depending on the plan, ranging from 2% to 5%.

What happens at the end of the term?

At the end of the term, your principal is automatically returned to your account balance. You can then withdraw it or reinvest in another plan.

Is there a maximum investment amount?

There is no maximum investment amount for our long-term plans. You can invest as much as you'd like above the minimum requirement.
